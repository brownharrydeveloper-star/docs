## What is RugPull?

RugPull is an AI-driven infrastructure that coordinates liquidity and execution to actively participate in memecoin market formation, rather than passively reacting to price movements.

## How does RugPull work?

RugPull deploys AI systems to create and manage memecoin markets. User-staked capital is pooled and used to provide liquidity for these markets, enabling controlled execution and coordinated price dynamics during trading activity.

## How are profits generated?

Profits are generated from coordinated liquidity management and execution during market activity. Rather than relying on speculation, returns come from participating in how the market operates.

## Who can participate?

Anyone can participate by contributing capital through rugpull.software. Rewards are distributed proportionally based on contribution weight.

## How are rewards distributed?

Rewards are distributed proportionally based on each user's contribution weight within the liquidity pool. The greater the contribution, the greater the share of generated returns.

## What is RugPull Network Insight?

RugPull Network Insight is a distributed network of nodes, routers, and validators that operates continuously to synchronize liquidity, manage market pressure, and sustain memecoin ecosystems at scale.

## Can I withdraw my capital anytime?

Capital may be subject to protocol-defined coordination periods depending on market conditions and strategy execution.

## Is RugPull a trading platform?

No. RugPull does not provide manual trading tools. It provides market infrastructure.

## How is RugPull different from traditional staking?

Traditional staking earns yield by waiting. RugPull generates returns by participating in market behavior itself, turning capital into an active component of price formation.
