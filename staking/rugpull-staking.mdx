## How to Stake

You can participate in staking by delegating your tokens to routers. The minimum time is 30 days, and rewards are distributed every second.

<Card title="Stake Now" icon="coins" href="https://rugpull.software/">
  Start staking at rugpull.software
</Card>

---

## 1. Select the asset you want to stake

Choose from the list of supported tokens available for staking.

<img src="/images/staking/staking-select-asset.png" alt="Select Asset" />

## 2. Select staking term

Choose your preferred staking duration. Minimum staking period is 30 days.

Select a funding source from your holdings, then enter the amount to stake.

## 5. Authorize Token Usage

This approval allows Rugpull to use your token for staking operations. You only need to approve once to avoid repeated transaction fees.

<Tip>
You can revoke this permission anytime from your wallet.
</Tip>

<img src="/images/staking/staking-authorize-token.png" alt="Authorize Token Usage" />
