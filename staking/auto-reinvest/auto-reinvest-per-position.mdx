---
title: 'Auto-reinvest (Per Position)'
description: 'Enable auto-reinvest for individual staking positions'
---

Each staking position includes an Auto-reinvest toggle in the Details view. When enabled, all claimable rewards from that position will be automatically reinvested into the same staking term.

<Info>
- Applies only to the selected position
- Rewards are reinvested in real time
- Can be turned on or off anytime
- Does not affect other positions
</Info>
