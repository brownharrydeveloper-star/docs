---
title: 'Auto-reinvest (All Positions)'
description: 'Enable auto-reinvest for all staking positions at once'
---

The Auto-all option allows you to enable or disable auto-reinvest for all active positions at once. This action updates the auto-reinvest setting across every eligible position.

<Info>
- Applies to all current active positions
- Future positions are not affected automatically
- Can be reverted anytime
- Individual toggles will reflect this change
</Info>

<img src="/images/auto-reinvest/auto-reinvest-all-positions.png" alt="Auto-reinvest All Positions" />

---

## When Auto-reinvest is OFF

Rewards will remain claimable and will no longer be compounded automatically.

---

## When Auto-reinvest is ON

When auto-reinvest is enabled, any new rewards generated after activation will be automatically reinvested and will not be available to claim.
