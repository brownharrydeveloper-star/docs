Overview of Rugpull.software reinvest mechanics

---

## How to Reinvest

You can participate in staking by delegating your tokens to routers. The minimum time is 30 days, and rewards are distributed every second.

<Card title="Stake Now" icon="coins" href="https://rugpull.software/">
  Start staking at rugpull.software
</Card>

---

## 1. Select the asset you want to stake

Choose from the list of supported tokens available for staking.

<img src="/images/reinvest/reinvest-select-asset.png" alt="Select Asset" />

---

## 2. Select staking term

Choose your preferred staking duration.

<img src="/images/reinvest/reinvest-select-term.png" alt="Select Staking Term" />

---

## 3. Select Funds to Reinvest

Select **Claimable** as the funding source, then enter the amount to stake.

<Info>
- **Claimable balance** represents rewards from previous staking.
- If selected, **100% of the claimable amount** will be staked. Partial staking is not supported.
</Info>

<img src="/images/reinvest/reinvest-select-funds.png" alt="Select Funds to Reinvest" />

---

## 4. Review & Confirm Reinvest

Review all details and confirm the reinvestment transaction.

<img src="/images/reinvest/reinvest-review-confirm.png" alt="Review and Confirm Reinvest" />
